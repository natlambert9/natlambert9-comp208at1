Hello and welcome to my professional teaching home page

My name is Nathaniel Moore-Lambert, and I am a pre-service teacher from Cronulla, pursuing a career as a dedicated Technology and Applied Studies teacher. I am in my fourth year of my course, Bachelor of Education (Secondary)/Bachelor of Arts (Technology) at Australian Catholic University, Strathfield.

I discovered my passion for the industrial arts in high school, especially in woodwork and graphics, my passion shines through in my projects and enthusiasm and I can't wait to invoke this same passion into my future students.

This website was created to provide you with an overview of my personal and professional development as a teacher.

Here you will find the confluence of my innovation, inspiration, knowledge, skills, and reflections that define my teaching journey, philosophies, and plans in order to flourish as a teacher in contemporary Australian society.


TAS subjects are not just lessons, they are gateways to equipping students with the practical skills, creative mindset, and problem-solving abilities they need to navigate the complexities of the modern world and shape a future filled with innovation and progress.
